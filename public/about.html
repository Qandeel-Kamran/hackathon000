Book Overview

This comprehensive guide covers the fundamentals of embodied intelligence, robot control systems, perception, and humanoid robotics.

About This Book

Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics is a comprehensive guide that explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems. This book combines theoretical concepts with practical examples and interactive learning tools to help students understand how physical systems can exhibit intelligent behavior.

Key Topics Covered

  • Embodied Intelligence Principles
  • Robot Kinematics and Dynamics
  • Control Systems and Algorithms
  • Sensor Integration and Perception
  • Humanoid Robot Design
  • Balance and Locomotion
  • ROS 2 Integration
  • Simulation and Real-World Applications

About the Author

This book is designed for students, researchers, and professionals interested in the fields of robotics, artificial intelligence, and embodied intelligence. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications to provide a comprehensive understanding of Physical AI and humanoid robotics systems.

The content has been developed with input from experts in robotics, AI, and education to ensure that it provides both foundational knowledge and advanced insights into the field.
